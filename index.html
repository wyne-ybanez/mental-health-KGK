About Us

We are providing an app where the user can track their psychological symptoms, enabling communication with mental health services who can offer help and advice, called ‘Connected’.

The data collected regarding user’s symptoms can be utilized by our team. With the adoption of machine learning algorithms and psychological analysis, we aim to provide highly personalized and catered solutions to our users.

App Functionality

Personal Mood Tracker

A personal and family perception mood tracker. Updated with the newest forms of psychographics, personality indicators and various mental health illnesses being applied to machine learning algorithms to provide catered advice for both you and your family. These algorithms will spot patterns in one’s life which may be negatively affecting the user and advise coping strategies.

Online Therapy

A secure forum for connecting those going through similar experiences. A therapy forum which can be linked to a user’s online therapist. This could be developed massively from the therapist's point of view. This could be developed massively from the therapist's point of view.

Customizability & Emergency Features

An individually customisable user experience will allow the user to have selected features on the home screen to provide ease of access. This could include setting up personal favourite stress coping content in the form of videos, music, or pictures. A ‘Red’ crisis button with access to emergency services
